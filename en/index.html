What’s the Nettieverse?

Nettieverse is all about celebrating forgotten women in science, those whose discoveries were often overlooked or credited to someone else. The name comes from Nettie Stevens, the geneticist who discovered the X and Y chromosomes, yet somehow didn’t get the recognition she deserved.

Through articles, profiles, and reviews of films and books, Nettieverse brings these brilliant women and their stories back into the spotlight. Our mission is simple: to give visibility to the women who changed science without ever getting the credit they deserved. Here, we talk about passion, curiosity, unfairness, but also hope and inspiration. Because before they were erased from history, these women were busy changing the world.
