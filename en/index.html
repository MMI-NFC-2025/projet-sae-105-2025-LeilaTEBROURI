Tribute to the Women Who Shaped History

What’s the Nettieverse?

Nettieverse celebrates the women scientists whose discoveries were overlooked or credited to someone else. The name honors Nettie Stevens, the geneticist who identified the X and Y chromosomes yet remained in the shadows.

Learn More

Through articles, portraits, and reviews of films or books, Nettieverse brings these researchers and their stories back into the spotlight. The goal is simple: make visible the women who transformed science without recognition. We talk about passion, curiosity, injustice, but also hope and inspiration. Before they were erased, these women were already changing the world.
